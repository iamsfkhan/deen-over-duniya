## ğŸ’ª Other Daily Sunnats

### 1. Tahajjud (Night Prayer)
Pray at least 2 Rakats before Fajr.

<div className="hadith-box">
  <div className="hadith-translation">
    The Prophet ï·º said: "The best prayer after the obligatory prayers is the night prayer."
  </div>
  <div className="hadith-reference">
    Sahih Muslim
  </div>
</div>

### 2. Duha Prayer
Pray between sunrise and Dhuhr (2, 4, 8, or 12 Rakats).

### 3. Smile
<div className="hadith-box">
  <div className="hadith-translation">
    The Prophet ï·º said: "Your smiling in the face of your brother is an act of charity."
  </div>
  <div className="hadith-reference">
    Sunan al-Tirmidhi
  </div>
</div>

### 4. Give Salam
Spread peace by greeting others.

<div className="hadith-box">
  <div className="hadith-translation">
    The Prophet ï·º said: "You will not enter Paradise until you believe, and you will not believe until you love one another. Shall I tell you of something that, if you do it, you will love one another? Spread Salam amongst yourselves."
  </div>
  <div className="hadith-reference">
    Sahih Muslim
  </div>
</div>

### 5. Make Dhikr
Remember Allah throughout the day:
- **SubhanAllah** (Glory be to Allah)
- **Alhamdulillah** (All praise is for Allah)
- **Allahu Akbar** (Allah is the Greatest)
- **La ilaha illallah** (There is no god but Allah)
